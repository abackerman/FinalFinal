Adrianne Ackerman

Graphic Designer

As a native of Long Island, New York, the move to an incredibly rural Saxapahaw, North Carolina played out as a unique experience that juxtaposed the affinities for one environmental factor against the other. My intimate attention to detail in this experience and beyond has unfurled a unique perspective within the world of design.
My personality made appearances throughout my formative years via various versions of visual communication. Accolades trailed behind my performances as a trained dancer; I took photographs; I painted and drew. Such passions drew me to psychology as I uncovered the reasons why and how we all make the decisions we make. With a natural ability to appreciate the visual arts, discovering graphic design became a welcome new opportunity for storytelling. I delved deep into this curiosity and have persistently pursued it ever since.
